ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
This Acceptable Use Policy (“AUP”) governs Customer’s use of the COMPLY‑QUICK LLC compliance‑automation Platform and Services. This AUP forms part of the Master Service Agreement (“MSA”), Terms of Service (“ToS”), and any applicable Order Forms.

By accessing or using the Platform, Customer agrees to comply with this AUP.

1. PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY
The purpose of this AUP is to ensure that all users of the COMPLY‑QUICK Platform:

Use the system responsibly

Maintain regulatory integrity

Avoid misuse of automated compliance tools

Protect the security and stability of the Platform

Prevent fraudulent or deceptive compliance practices

This AUP applies to all Authorized Users, employees, contractors, and third parties accessing the Platform under Customer’s account.

2. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES
Customer and Authorized Users may not engage in any of the following activities:

2.1 Fraudulent or Misleading Compliance Practices
Users may not:

Generate compliance clauses for the purpose of deceiving regulators, auditors, clients, or the public

Use Outputs to falsely claim compliance, certification, or regulatory approval

Fabricate audit evidence or regulatory documentation

Misrepresent automated Outputs as legal advice or attorney‑reviewed content

Use the Platform to create fraudulent contracts, policies, or attestations

2.2 Illegal Activities
Users may not use the Platform to:

Violate U.S. federal, Louisiana state, or international laws

Facilitate money laundering, fraud, or other criminal activity

Upload or distribute illegal content

Assist in evading regulatory obligations

2.3 Security Violations
Users may not:

Attempt unauthorized access to the Platform

Circumvent authentication or security controls

Probe, scan, or test system vulnerabilities

Deploy malware, ransomware, or malicious scripts

Interfere with Platform performance or stability

Attempt to access data belonging to other customers

2.4 Misuse of Regulatory Datasets
Users may not:

Scrape, copy, or extract regulatory datasets

Redistribute Provider’s proprietary regulatory mappings

Use automated tools to harvest legal frameworks or clause libraries

Reverse engineer Provider’s regulatory intelligence systems

2.5 Misuse of AI‑Generated Outputs
Users may not:

Present Outputs as guaranteed compliance

Use Outputs without human review

Claim that COMPLY‑QUICK LLC certifies or endorses compliance status

Use Outputs to bypass legal counsel or regulatory oversight

2.6 Platform Abuse
Users may not:

Overload the system with excessive automated requests

Use bots or scripts to generate mass Outputs

Attempt to degrade system performance

Use the Platform in ways that interfere with other customers

2.7 Intellectual Property Violations
Users may not:

Copy, modify, or create derivative works of the Platform

Reverse engineer or decompile any part of the system

Remove copyright notices or proprietary markings

Use Provider’s trademarks without permission

3. CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES
Customer agrees to:

3.1 Maintain Account Security
Protect login credentials

Use strong passwords

Enable multi‑factor authentication where available

Monitor Authorized User activity

3.2 Ensure Authorized User Compliance
Customer is responsible for ensuring all Authorized Users comply with this AUP.

3.3 Validate Outputs
Customer must review and validate all Outputs before use.

3.4 Report Violations
Customer must promptly report suspected violations to:
security@comply‑quick.com

4. DATA AND CONTENT RESTRICTIONS
Customer may not upload, store, or transmit:

Sensitive personal data without proper legal basis

Protected health information (PHI) unless authorized

Classified or government‑restricted data

Copyrighted materials without permission

Content that is defamatory, obscene, or harmful

5. SYSTEM INTEGRATION RESTRICTIONS
Customer may not:

Integrate the Platform with unauthorized third‑party systems

Use APIs in ways that exceed rate limits

Attempt to bypass API authentication

Use the Platform to build competing regulatory datasets

6.2 Temporary Suspension

6.3 Permanent Termination
Termination of Customer’s account for severe or repeated violations.

6.4 Legal Action

Security breaches

Intellectual property violations

6.5 Cooperation with Authorities
COMPLY‑QUICK LLC may cooperate with law enforcement when violations involve illegal activity.
