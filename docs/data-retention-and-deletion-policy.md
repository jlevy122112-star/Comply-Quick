This Data Retention & Deletion Policy (“Policy”) describes how COMPLY‑QUICK LLC (“Company,” “We,” “Us,” “Our”) stores, retains, archives, and deletes Customer Data and Personal Information processed through the COMPLY‑QUICK compliance‑automation Platform.

This Policy applies to all users of the Platform and is designed to meet U.S. federal requirements, Louisiana state law, industry standards, and enterprise procurement expectations.

1. PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY
The purpose of this Policy is to:

Define how long different categories of data are retained

Describe how data is archived and deleted

Ensure compliance with legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations

Provide transparency to customers, auditors, and regulators

Establish secure data‑handling practices

This Policy forms part of the Master Service Agreement (MSA), Terms of Service (ToS), and Data Processing Agreement (DPA).

2. DEFINITIONS
2.1 “Customer Data”
All data uploaded, submitted, transmitted, or integrated by Customer into the Platform, including documents, regulatory inputs, workflows, and metadata.

2.2 “Personal Information”
Any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual.

2.3 “System Logs”
Operational logs generated by the Platform, including authentication logs, API logs, workflow execution logs, and audit logs.

2.4 “Backup Data”
Encrypted copies of Customer Data stored for disaster recovery and business continuity.

2.5 “Deletion”
The irreversible removal of data from active systems, backups, and archives.

3. GENERAL RETENTION PRINCIPLES
COMPLY‑QUICK LLC retains data only for as long as necessary to:

Provide Services

Comply with legal obligations

Resolve disputes

Enforce agreements

Maintain security and auditability

Retention periods vary by data type and regulatory requirements.

4. RETENTION PERIODS BY DATA CATEGORY
4.1 Customer‑Uploaded Documents
Examples: policies, procedures, contracts, regulatory evidence
Retention Period: Retained for the duration of the Customer’s active subscription.
Deletion: Within 30 days of account termination or deletion request.

4.2 Compliance Outputs Generated by the Platform
Examples: clauses, workflows, mappings, audit reports
Retention Period: Retained for the duration of the Customer’s active subscription.
Deletion: Within 30 days of account termination or deletion request.

4.3 Account Information
Examples: name, email, company details, billing information
Retention Period: Retained while the account is active.
Post‑Termination: Retained for 7 years for tax, audit, and legal compliance.

4.4 System Logs
Examples: authentication logs, API logs, workflow logs
Retention Period:

Security logs: 12 months

Audit logs: 24 months

Operational logs: 6 months

4.5 Support Tickets & Communications
Examples: emails, chat logs, support requests
Retention Period: 3 years  
Purpose: Quality assurance, dispute resolution, compliance.

4.6 Payment Records
Examples: invoices, transaction logs
Retention Period: 7 years  
Purpose: Tax and financial compliance.

4.7 Backup Data
Retention Period: 30–90 days, depending on backup cycle.
Deletion: Automatically purged after retention period.

4.8 AI Model Training Data
COMPLY‑QUICK LLC does not use identifiable Customer Data for global model training unless:

Customer provides explicit consent

Data is anonymized and aggregated
Retention Period: As defined in consent documentation.

5.2 Deletion Timeline

5.3 Secure Deletion Methods
COMPLY‑QUICK LLC uses:

Cryptographic erasure

Secure overwrite procedures

Automated deletion workflows

Backup purge cycles

Deletion is irreversible.

6. ACCOUNT TERMINATION
When a Customer terminates their account:

6.1 Data Export Option
Customer may request export of:

Audit logs

6.2 Automatic Deletion

Account credentials removed immediately

Logs retained per retention schedule
