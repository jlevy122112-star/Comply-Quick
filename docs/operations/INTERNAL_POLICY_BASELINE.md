# Comply-Quick Internal Policy Baseline (White-Label)

This baseline defines the minimum internal policy set required for enterprise-grade legal and compliance operations.

## Required Policies

1. Information Security Policy
2. Access Control Policy
3. Data Classification and Handling Policy
4. Data Retention and Deletion Policy
5. Encryption and Key Management Policy
6. Incident Response and Breach Notification Policy
7. Vendor Risk Management Policy
8. Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Policy
9. Change Management Policy
10. Monitoring and Logging Policy
11. Security Awareness and Training Policy
12. Secure SDLC Policy

## Policy Ownership Model

| Policy | Primary Owner | Review Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Security + Access + Monitoring | Founder / Engineering Lead | Quarterly |
| Incident + Breach + BCDR | Founder / Incident Commander | Quarterly and post-incident |
| Vendor + DPA + Subprocessors | Founder / Legal Ops | Quarterly |
| Retention + DSAR + Privacy | Founder / Privacy Ops | Quarterly |
| Training + SDLC + Change | Founder / Engineering Lead | Quarterly |

## Evidence Expectations

- Policies versioned with effective date.
- Review date recorded at each review cycle.
- Control evidence linked in Notion HQ Compliance Evidence Register.
- Material changes reflected in external legal docs where applicable.

## Counsel Review Status

- Drafted for legal counsel review.
- Not a substitute for jurisdiction-specific legal advice.
- Final enforceability requires counsel sign-off.
