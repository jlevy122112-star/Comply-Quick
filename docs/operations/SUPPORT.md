# Support Policy

How customers reach Comply-Quick for help, what to expect, and how issues
escalate. Response-time targets by plan live in `SLA.md`.

## 1. Channels

| Channel | Address / location | Best for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Email | `support@comply-quick.com` | All support requests, the system of record. |
| General enquiries | `info@comply-quick.com` | Sales / non-technical questions. |
| In-app | Dashboard (help/contact) | Context-attached reports from signed-in users. |

There is no Slack support channel (Slack was removed from the product); email is
the primary, tracked channel.

## 2. Hours

- **Standard (Free / Pro / Agency):** business hours, Monday–Friday, replies per
  the `SLA.md` targets.
- **Enterprise / dedicated support:** extended coverage including 24/7 for Sev-1,
  a named point of contact, and a private escalation path agreed in the order
  form.

## 3. What to include in a report

To get the fastest resolution, include: the affected org/workspace, the URL or
feature, what you expected vs. saw, timestamps (with time zone), and any error
message or request ID. For suspected security issues, do **not** post details
publicly — email `support@comply-quick.com` with "SECURITY" in the subject.

## 4. Escalation

1. Support triages and assigns a severity (`INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md` §1).
2. Sev-1/Sev-2 are escalated to on-call immediately and, if a fault within our
   control, become incidents.
3. Enterprise customers may escalate through their named contact per their
   agreement.

## 5. Dedicated support (Enterprise)

The Enterprise "dedicated support" offering includes:

- A named account/support contact.
- Priority response targets (see `SLA.md`).
- Onboarding assistance (org/workspace setup, SSO/SAML/OIDC and SCIM
  configuration).
- A private escalation channel for Sev-1 incidents.

## 6. Security disclosure

We welcome responsible disclosure. Email `support@comply-quick.com` (subject
"SECURITY"). We will acknowledge, investigate, and — where a personal-data breach
is confirmed — follow `INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md` and the in-product breach workflow.
Please give us reasonable time to remediate before public disclosure.
