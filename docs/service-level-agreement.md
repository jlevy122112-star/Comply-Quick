SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
This Service Level Agreement (“SLA”) forms part of the Master Service Agreement (“MSA”) between:

COMPLY‑QUICK LLC, a Louisiana limited liability company headquartered in Jennings, Louisiana, USA (“Provider”),
and
The entity or individual subscribing to the COMPLY‑QUICK Platform (“Customer”).

This SLA defines the service levels, performance commitments, support obligations, and remedies applicable to Customer’s use of the COMPLY‑QUICK compliance‑automation Platform.

1. DEFINITIONS
1.1 “Platform”
The COMPLY‑QUICK LLC cloud‑based RegTech SaaS system, including clause generation, regulatory mapping, workflow automation, audit documentation, APIs, dashboards, and related services.

1.2 “Scheduled Maintenance”
Planned downtime for updates, improvements, or infrastructure work.

1.3 “Unscheduled Downtime”
Unexpected service interruptions not caused by Scheduled Maintenance.

1.4 “Monthly Uptime Percentage”
The percentage of total minutes in a calendar month during which the Platform is fully operational and accessible.

1.5 “Service Credit”
A credit applied to Customer’s account as a remedy for failure to meet uptime commitments.

1.6 “Support Request”
Any request submitted by Customer for assistance, troubleshooting, or issue resolution.

2. SERVICE AVAILABILITY
2.1 Uptime Commitment
COMPLY‑QUICK LLC will make the Platform available 99.5% of the time each calendar month, excluding Scheduled Maintenance and Excluded Downtime.

2.2 Scheduled Maintenance
Scheduled Maintenance will be performed during low‑traffic hours.

Provider will give 48 hours’ notice for planned maintenance windows.

Scheduled Maintenance will not exceed 8 hours per month.

2.3 Excluded Downtime
Downtime caused by the following does not count against uptime:

Scheduled Maintenance

Force Majeure events

Customer misuse or configuration errors

Third‑party service failures outside Provider’s control

Internet or network outages external to Provider

Security incidents caused by Customer

Violations of the MSA or ToS

Customer’s refusal to install required updates

3. SERVICE MONITORING
COMPLY‑QUICK LLC continuously monitors:

Platform uptime

API availability

System performance

Error rates

Latency

Infrastructure health

Monitoring is performed using industry‑standard tools and internal systems.

4. SUPPORT SERVICES
4.1 Support Channels
Customer may request support via:

Email: support@comply‑quick.com

In‑Platform support portal

Ticketing system

Emergency hotline (enterprise plans only)

4.2 Support Hours
Standard Support: Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–6:00 PM CST

Emergency Support: 24/7 (enterprise plans only)

4.3 Response Time Commitments
Support requests are categorized as follows:

Critical Priority
Definition: Platform unavailable, severe outage, data loss, or major security incident.
Response Time: Within 4 hours.
Resolution Target: Continuous work until resolved.

High Priority
Definition: Major functionality impaired; significant business impact.
Response Time: Within 12 hours.
Resolution Target: 1–3 business days.

Medium Priority
Definition: Non‑critical functionality issues; moderate impact.
Response Time: Within 24 hours.
Resolution Target: 3–7 business days.

Low Priority
Definition: Minor issues, cosmetic defects, general inquiries.
Response Time: Within 48 hours.
Resolution Target: As scheduled in development cycle.

5.1 API Performance
99.5% availability

Average response time under 500ms

Rate limits defined in Order Form

5.2 Platform Performance
Page load times under 2 seconds under normal conditions

Clause generation under 5 seconds for standard inputs

Workflow execution under 1 second per step

5.3 Data Processing
Real‑time processing for most operations

Batch processing for large imports

Audit logs updated within 60 seconds

6. SECURITY COMMITMENTS
COMPLY‑QUICK LLC maintains:

Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+)

Encryption at rest (AES‑256)

Multi‑factor authentication

Network segmentation

Intrusion detection

Continuous monitoring

Regular penetration testing

Incident response procedures

Secure development lifecycle

Security obligations are further detailed in the MSA and DPA.

7. SERVICE CREDITS
7.1 Eligibility
If Monthly Uptime Percentage falls below 99.5%, Customer is eligible for Service Credits.

7.2 Credit Amounts
Monthly Uptime	Service Credit
99.0%–99.49%	5% of monthly fee
98.0%–98.99%	10% of monthly fee
95.0%–97.99%	25% of monthly fee
Below 95.0%	50% of monthly fee


7.3 Claim Process
Customer must submit a claim within 30 days of the incident.
Claims must include:

Date and time of outage

Description of impact

Logs or evidence (if available)

7.4 Limitations
Service Credits:

Are Customer’s sole remedy for downtime

Cannot exceed monthly subscription fees

Do not apply to free or trial accounts

8. CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES
Customer must:

Maintain secure credentials

Use supported browsers and systems

Follow Provider’s configuration guidelines

Install required updates

Avoid misuse or unauthorized access

Provide accurate information during support requests

9. EXCLUSIONS
This SLA does not apply to:

Beta features

Free plans

Third‑party integrations

Customer‑hosted environments

Custom code or modifications

Issues caused by Customer’s infrastructure

Natural disasters

Labor disputes

Pandemic‑related disruptions
