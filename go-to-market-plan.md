
- **Product Overview (GTM Context)**  
  Comply-Quick is a compliance SaaS that scans websites for GDPR, CCPA, ADA, and HIPAA risks, then automatically creates tech-stack-specific legal documents like waivers, privacy policies, and checklists. It stands out from competitors such as Termly, CookieYes, Osano, and iubenda by offering tech-stack awareness, a scan-first workflow, agency white-label options, and a compliance marketplace. Pricing tiers range from Free to Solo ($29/mo), Agency ($99/mo), and Enterprise ($299/mo).

- **Target Customer Segments (ICPs)**  
  1. Solo Freelancer/Web Developer: Builds 2–20 client sites yearly on platforms like Shopify, Webflow, WordPress, WooCommerce; needs fast compliant sites without hiring lawyers. Channels: Twitter/X dev communities, Reddit, YouTube tutorials. Entry: Free scan leading to Solo plan upgrade.  
  2. Digital/Web Agency: 5–50 staff managing 10–100+ client sites with varied tech stacks; requires unified compliance and audit automation. Channels: Agency forums, LinkedIn ads, partner referrals. Entry: Agency plan trial with white-label and dashboard features.  
  3. Compliance Consultant/Legal Tech Buyer: Creates specialized compliance templates; seeks scalable monetization. Channels: LinkedIn, legal tech newsletters, communities. Entry: Marketplace creator account.  
  4. Regulated Vertical SMB: Industries like healthcare, fintech, education, e-commerce needing ongoing compliance monitoring. Channels: Industry associations, LinkedIn outbound, SEO content. Entry: Enterprise plan via demos and inbound leads.

- **Positioning & Messaging**  
  - Primary Positioning: "The only compliance platform that scans your site's actual tech stack and auto-generates all necessary legal documents within a minute."  
  - Differentiators include scan-first detection, stack-aware documents, agency white-label, regulation autopilot, compliance marketplace, and embeddable score badges.  
  - Taglines for A/B testing emphasize speed, cost savings, and agency support.

- **Pricing Strategy**  
  - Free plan offers 1 scan/month as an entry point.  
  - Solo plan ($29/mo) targets freelancers with 20 scans/month.  
  - Agency plan ($99/mo) includes 5 seats and 100 scans, focusing on agencies.  
  - Enterprise plan ($299/mo) offers unlimited scans for regulated verticals.  
  - Tactics include a 7-day free Agency trial, annual billing discounts, clear overage pricing, and a Starter Agency plan at $49/mo.

- **Distribution Channels (Free-First Approach)**  
  - Product-Led Growth: Free scans as main CTA, embeddable compliance badges, public score pages, marketplace templates, and partner referral programs.  
  - Content SEO: Target high-intent commercial keywords, problem-aware informational content, and competitor comparisons to drive organic traffic.  
  - Community & Outreach: Engage on Reddit, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Product Hunt, Hacker News, and agency newsletters.  
  - Partnership Channel: Collaborate with agency SaaS partners, legal tech consultants, hosting platforms, and accountants/bookkeepers.

- **Launch Sequence (90-Day GTM Plan)**  
  - Days 1–14: Set up analytics, recruit beta users, define key user moments.  
  - Days 15–30: Publish SEO content, start social and community engagement, activate referral program.  
  - Days 31–60: Launch on Product Hunt and Hacker News, run free audit week, outreach to newsletters, start affiliate campaigns.  
  - Days 61–90: Test paid ads, A/B test landing pages, promote compliance badges, pursue SaaS integrations, publish case studies.

- **Conversion & Retention Funnel**  
  - Acquisition to Activation: Free scan with no friction; "aha moment" when scan reveals real risks.  
  - Activation to Retention: Autopilot weekly rescans and alerts, compliance calendar, NPS surveys.  
  - Retention to Expansion: Seat upgrades, clear overage pricing, marketplace upsells, annual billing incentives.  
  - Expansion to Advocacy: Compliance badges and shareable score pages drive referrals; partner dashboard offers revenue share.

- **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**  
  - Targets for free signups, conversion rates, monthly recurring revenue, retention, NPS, organic traffic, partner referrals, agency accounts, and LTV:CAC ratio over 30, 90, and 365 days.
